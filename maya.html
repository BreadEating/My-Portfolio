Maya Animation Demo

An animation created in Maya, showcasing a mystery scene on the moon. This project highlights my skills in animation and modeling, with a focus on motion capture and fine tunes afterwards.

About This Project

This project was created as part of a class assignment, where we were tasked with creating a short animation using Maya. The goal was to demonstrate our understanding of 3D modeling, animation techniques, and the use of motion capture data. The final animation showcases a mystery scene on the moon, blending humor and suspense.

  • Tools: Maya 2025
  • Duration: 4 Days
  • Render: 24fps MP4, 720p
